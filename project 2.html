My favorite bikes

The love of the motorcycle is hard to understand for those who don’t ride and may well become a passion for those that do. If you ask a serious bike what his bike represents it is often the word “Freedom” that will emerge. The truth is that biking provides a sense of exhilaration that few other modes of transport can. Biking gangs attest to this sense of freedom in that many of them feel unconstrained by the rules of society…
